UTMA at a glance

The University of Traditional Medicine of Armenia (UTMA) is one of the world’s leading universities in traditional, complementary and alternative medicine. UTMA creates an exceptional Medicine, Stomatology and TCAM learning environment, that fosters healthy and bouncing society and supports outstanding research to discover best methods of diseases treatment, to serve the people around the world.


UTM of Armenia provides the opportunity for transformative student learning through outstanding teaching and research, enriched educational experiences and also University creates and advances knowledge and understanding, and improves the quality of life through the discovery, dissemination, and application of research within and across disciplines.
