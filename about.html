Who We Are

Edutrack Educational Consultants, incepted in 2009, is a Kerala-based Educational Consultancy, Promoted by an expert team of professionals providing best solutions to all students. We have got affiliations to conduct admissions in more than 100 colleges throughout our country and outside. We help the student identify the right university and the right program.

Edutrack keeps extensive knowledge of ever-changing education sector. With the valuable insights to deliver, we offer the best educational, cultural and financial solutions to the undergraduates, graduates and all the interested students. Our team comprises the potential educators and counselling experts, along with a huge Alumni network to refer you to the top colleges and universities, we also provide support to education loan, visa processing, counselling to students to achieve their goal From form filling to fees submission, visa processing, accommodation from the thought of studying abroad – we will help you at every step.
