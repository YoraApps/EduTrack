Why Armenia?

Cost Effective

When we talk about a country that provides the best possible facilities in all sectors, you might feel that such a country would be too expensive to live in. That’s where you are wrong. The cost of living is very low and budget friendly.


Attractive Fee Structure

Studying MBBS in many countries is very expensive. The cost of MBBS in Armenia however is very low. The MBBS in Armenia fee is very affordable leaving students free not to worry about financial matters.


Globally Recognized Medical Degree

The Armenian medical degree is globally recognized and is listed by many International bodies such as World Health Organization, European Union, The International Medical Education Directory, Medical and Dental council of India and General Medical Council UK. Also, the MBBS colleges in Armenia provide training for USMLE and other medical licensing examinations. The Armenian medical colleges are recognized by the MCI. The students who graduate from Armenia will be allowed to sit for the MCI screening test.


High Quality Education

The MBBS colleges in Armenia combine theoretical and practical learning in such a way that students are taught in the most effective way possible. All the professors who teach medicine in Armenia are highly trained to deal with international students. The use of innovative teaching aids and modern instruments make the quality of education in Armenia very high. The instruction medium is in English allowing international students to concentrate on learning medicine rather than learning a new language.


Quality Hospitals for Internships

From the second year onwards, students studying medicine in Armenia are eligible to attend internships. The hospitals affiliated to the medical colleges in Armenia provide top quality health care and use the latest medical technologies and instruments. These hospitals are also capable of housing a large number of students and patients.


Good Accommodation

Separate hostels are provided for international students with hot and cold water. Central heating is also provided in all hostels. All types of vegetarian and non-vegetarian dishes are also provided. All this at a very low cost.

Admission Procedure

The procedure for admission in the Under-Graduate Medical courses in Armenia for the Traditional Medical University will take place in 5 stages, as mentioned below.


  • Stage 1

    • All the details about the admission and the admission process shall be clearly explained to the students/parents as per their desired programme, along with the Fee Structure and Admission Charges and a Contract shall be got signed between Campus International LLC, and the student/student’s parents to initiate the admission process. At this stage, the first part payment is to be received from the students/parents and deposited in the bank account of Campus International LLC to take at this stage:

    • • 10th original mark sheet of the student
    • • 12th original mark sheet of the student
    • • 3 colored photocopies of the valid passport (minimum 1-year validity) of the student (without immigration requirement)
    • • Birth Certificate
    • • Biodata
    • • One passport photo with white background
    • • Medical fitness certificate from Govt regd. Dr
    • • CV of the student

    These above-mentioned documents will then be sent to us via mail to initiate the process of receiving the Admission Confirmation Letter from the University. The students/parents shall be asked to get the following medical tests done of the concerned student and submit the reports (in original) within a week, preferably when they will come to collect the Admission Confirmation Letter:

    • • HIV
    • • Malaria
    • • Up-to date Chicken Pox and Small Pox Vaccination status

    After we shall receive the first part payment, within a time of approx. 10 working days, we shall provide an ’Admission Confirmation Letter’, issued from the respective university in the student’s name after the due diligence and apostille of documents in India and in the university in Yerevan.


  • Stage 2

    • This stage shall be initiated with the student/parent once the Admission Confirmation Letter is received by the student from us. At this stage, the second part payment is to be received from the student/parent and deposited in our bank account. Documents to take at this stage:

    • • Original Valid Passport of the student with a minimum validity of 1 year (without immigration requirement)
    • • Medical Reports of the student (HIV, Malaria, Up-to date Chicken Pox and Small Pox Vaccination status) in original

      • The above-mentioned documents will then be sent to us in original for the Visa process to begin.

      After the second part payment and the documents mentioned above are successfully received from the student/parent, the Admission Confirmation Letter is to be given to the student/parent for his/their reference. A copy of the Admission Confirmation Letter receipt taken from the student/parent shall be kept by our Associate.

      Counsellor and the Xerox of the same shall be sent to us for our reference. After we receive the second part payment, within a maximum period of 35 to 40 days from then, we will get the Visa affixed to the student’s passport and hand it over to the Associate Counsellor. The Visa shall be valid for a period of 120 days, beginning from the day it is issued from the Embassy of Armenia. Within these 120 days, the resident card shall be created in the name of the student for a period of one year, after the student shall reach Armenia and shall complete all the formalities related to the said Resident Card.

  • Stage 3

    • This stage shall be initiated with the student/parent once the Passport with Visa affixed on it has been received by our Associate Counsellor from us.

    At this stage, the third part payment/full & final payment is to be received from the student/parent and deposited in our bank account.

    After this payment is successfully received by us, we shall send the Passport with Visa affixed on it for our Associate Counsellor to hand it over to the student along with his original documents (if any).

  • Stage 4

    • Eventually, our senior representative shall visit the airport to see off the students on the date of departure and meet our students/parents to provide them an added comfort and to take care of any minor issues which may have still been left unattended. Our senior representative will pick up students from airport and take them to the hostel.

  • Stage 5

    • On student’s arrival in Yerevan, there shall be a shuttle service available at the airport, free of cost, arranged by our Campus International LLC office which would assist the students in reaching their respective hostel accommodations in Yerevan, Armenia.

    Upon reaching Armenia, the process for the following documentation in student’s name shall be initiated by our Campus International LLC office there

    • • Armenian Resident Card (shall be renewed annually by us at no extra cost till the entire duration of the course). A medical test shall be conducted in Armenia for completing the process of getting their Resident Card.
    • • Medical Insurance for the first year (which has to be renewed annually). We can assist the student is required in obtaining the desired Medical Insurance package for consecutive years at the expenses incurred, as per actuals, of the package)
    • • University’s ID Card
    • • Documents translation & Notary
